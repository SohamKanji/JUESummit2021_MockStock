About The Event

Mock Stock is a 3 week long event where you will trade or invest in the top 100 companies of India, the one with the highest net worth at the end of the 3 weeks will win. A leaderboard will be maintained and updated in real time. Remember:

“The key to making money in stocks is not to get scared out of them.”

Do you have what it takes to be the The Wolf of Dalal Street?
